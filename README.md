# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analysis

## Project Overview  
  -  This project presents an interactive Sales Dashboard built using Microsoft Excel to analyze Coffee shop data. The primary goal is to create a dynamic sales            dashboard that provides valuable insights into sales performance, helping businesses understand trends and make informed decisions.
## Dataset Information  
The Dataset used in this project includes the following headers:  
  -  Transaction ID
  -  Transaction Date & Time
  -  Store Location
  -  Product Category
  -  Product Type & Details
  -  Quantity Sold
  -  Unit Price
  -  Total Amount
## Dashboard Highlights  
#### 1) Sales by Store Location - Pie Chart:
  -  Description: This chart displays the distribution of sales across different store location in percentage. The pie chart helps in to know about what proportion of sales is hold by each store location.
#### 2) Sales by Product types - Area Chart:    
  -  Description: This charts show the sales of top 7 product types, providing a visual representation of a steady increase in sales across of different product types, with espresso-based products performing better than regular brewed beverages.
#### 3) Quantity of Categories- Column Chart:  
  -  Description: This Column charts represents the sum of Quantity sold by product categories, each bar represents a specific category, and the height of the bar indicates the total quantity sold.
#### 4) Sales and Quantity by product category - Combo Charts:   
  -  Description: This combo charts displays the total sales and quantities of diferent product category. The chart helps in identify which product categories generate the most revenue and customer purchases.

## Tools Used  
  -  Microsoft Excel: For data cleaning, pre-proccessing, analysis, and visualization.
## Key Insights  
  -  The june month has the highest sales among all the month.
  -  Hell's kitchen store performed better than other two store.
  -  The coffee and tea category has the highest sales revenue.
  -  Most customer liked to buy barista espresso product.
